What's New

[Nov 6, 2017] ReproZip will be presented at Moore-Sloan Data Science Summit.
[Jul 13, 2017] ReproZip will be presented at SciPy 2017.
[Jul 10, 2017] ReproZip v1.0.10 has been released. Fixes reprozip combine crash on Python 3.6 (patch from James Clarke). New reprozip-jupyter tool to trace and reproduce Jupyter notebooks (can be registered as Jupyter extension for web interface).
[May 23, 2017] ReproZip will be presented at IASSIST 2017.
[May 1, 2017] ReproZip will be presented at PresQT Workshop One.
[Apr 24, 2017] ReproZip will be presented at the European Geosciences Union Meeting.
[Jan 26, 2017] ReproZip will be presented at Brainhack NYC.
[Jan 10, 2017] ReproZip v1.0.9 has been released. Fixes some issues with pyc files, non-standard resolv.conf, and files opened in read-write mode.
[Dec 02, 2016] Our paper was accepted at JOSS! Check it out!
[Oct 07, 2016] ReproZip v1.0.8 has been released. Adds a Qt GUI, support for docker-native, file filters, and VirtualBox display.
[Aug 22, 2016] ReproZip v1.0.7 has been released. Adds missing message if ptrace fails, faster setup for Docker and Vagrant, and support for RPM.
[July 27, 2016] ReproZip will be demoed at NYC Media Lab 2016.
[July 01, 2016] ReproZip will be presented at the PASIG Fall 2016 meeting.
[Jun 25, 2016] ReproZip v1.0.6 has been released. Adds docker --detach, reprozip combine, and faster metadata access with reprounzip info.
[Apr 07, 2016] ReproZip v1.0.5 has been released. Fixes download of parameters from our server.
[Mar 10, 2016] ReproZip v1.0.4 has been released. Uses requests, downloads parameters from our server, tweaks run/download CLI, and adds --docker-option
[Mar 10, 2016] We now have a GitHub repository with examples of ReproZip packages! Check it out!
[Mar 02, 2016] The ReproZip team has been invited to participate in Container Strategies for Data & Software Preservation that Promote Open Science, a workshop held at Notre Dame by the NSF-funded DASPOS team.
[Feb 27, 2016] ReproZip will be demoed at SIGMOD 2016.
[Jan 27, 2016] ReproZip will be presented at FORCE2016.
[Dec 02, 2015] ReproZip v1.0.3 has been released. Bugfixes; adds cleanup if unpack fails, and --set-env and --pass-env options.
[Nov 06, 2015] ReproZip is listed on the Artifact Evaluation Process guidelines.
[Oct 26, 2015] ReproZip v1.0.2 has been released. Docker and Vagrant fixes.
[June, 2015] ReproZip is the recommended tool for the Reproducibility Section of the Information Systems Journal, Elsevier.
[May, 2015] ReproZip is the recommended tool for the ACM SIGMOD 2015 Reproducibility Review.
